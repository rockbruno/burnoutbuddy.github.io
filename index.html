Don't let your phone
control your life.

Do you feel that you spend more time on apps like social media than you should? Feeling that you should stop but finding yourself unable to?

Burnout Buddy is a personal app blocker for iOS that is designed to help you break free from addictive apps and heal their impact on your mental health. Simply select the apps and websites you find harmful, configure the conditions in which you don't want to use them, and let Burnout Buddy do the rest. Try it and see the benefits for yourself; it's free and requires no account.

The end of doomscrolling

Burnout Buddy is stronger than your iPhone's native Screen Time controls. Designed specifically with social media addiction and unhealthy work habits in mind, it offers a wider variety of blocking mechanisms and allows you to be extremely specific regarding how access to a specific group of apps and websites should be restricted. Looking to block social media during the mornings with no option to snooze, then allow it during lunch only to block again during the night, back it up with your own custom-made Siri Shortcut activation trigger all while having other sets of apps follow completely different rules? Schemas allow you to do that and much more.

If you already use Burnout Buddy, keep an eye out for updates that add even more blocking mechanisms and customization points. You can see what we have in our roadmap further below and we're always open to feature requests!

Completely private

Everything you do with Burnout Buddy is stored locally on your device. Not only do we have no idea what you're restricting, but we also wouldn't be able to know even if we wanted to. All information regarding the content installed on your phone is made anonymous by your iPhone and is accessible by Burnout Buddy only in the form of anonymous identifiers.

What can you do right now?

Here are the features that Burnout Buddy currently supports.

Schedule Conditions

Blocks content via a highly customizable schedule.

Usage Conditions

Blocks content when a daily usage limit is reached.

Shortcuts Condition (Custom)

Blocks content when requested by Siri or the Shortcuts app, allowing you to design your own custom blocking conditions by integrating Burnout Buddy with other apps and features of your device!

Composable Conditions

Configure multiple blocking conditions for the same schema for maximum control of your habits.

Multiple Schemas

Looking to block a certain group of apps under a certain condition but also another group under another condition? We got you covered.

Strict Mode

Have the bad habit of "cheating" app blockers by snoozing them? Strict Mode bans you from updating/deleting schemas when they're active.

Starter Schemas

Plug-and-play configurations that cover the most common reasons people use Burnout Buddy for.

Custom App Icons

Personalize your home screen with an icon that better matches your style!

Push Notifications

Receive alerts when one of your schema's condition triggers and/or untriggers.

Frequently asked questions

What black magic is this? Did you just hack my iPhone???

Burnout Buddy is possible thanks to a set of new self-control app-blocking features introduced by Apple in iOS 16. That's why you need iOS 16 to run Burnout Buddy!

Can I use Burnout Buddy on macOS?

You can download Burnout Buddy for macOS here, but it contains significantly fewer features than the iOS counterpart and is not in active development right now. Once Apple releases the Screen Time APIs for macOS we'll be able to distribute the real thing in the macOS App Store.

(Schedule Conditions) Why are the schedules in 15-minute intervals?

The aforementioned APIs for app blocking are a bit limited right now, but we're working with Apple to improve them and make Burnout Buddy even more powerful!
