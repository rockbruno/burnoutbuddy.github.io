Burnout Buddy

Support

As you might know by now, BB is largely powered by your phone's built-in app blocking systems, which are provided and maintained by Apple themselves.

Unfortunately, Apple doesn't seem to be interested in this area anymore. Despite being full of limitations and bugs, they haven't updated those systems in over 2 years. They haven't even bothered to reply to any of the bug reports and improvement requests I sent them, many of which came from awesome messages you guys sent me that I really appreciate.

What this means is that if you're here to propose a feature or report a problem, there's a very good chance there's nothing I can do about it. It's up to Apple to improve their systems, and as of writing it doesn't seem like they will do it anytime soon.

I do encourage you however to e-mail me if you're facing an issue that you believe it's the app's fault. Even if it turns out it's Apple fault (it most likely will be), these reports can result in me pushing updates to make the UI easier to use / understand, so they're still very helpful. But before you do, here are answers to frequently asked questions:

Frequently Asked Questions

Issues involving the app selection screen

This screen is a feature of iOS, not Burnout Buddy. I have reported the issues to Apple and there's not much that can be done other than hope that they will fix it in an iOS update.

Processing takes a very long time, or never finishes

This usually happens when using beta versions of iOS. It's normal for beta versions to break apps and I do not recommend using them on your main device. Otherwise, it's something I have also reported to Apple and can't do much about other than hope they will improve it. Generally if you face this issue all you have to do is wait (even if it takes a very long time).

Anything not working when using iOS Betas

It's normal for beta versions to break apps and I do not recommend using them on your main device.
